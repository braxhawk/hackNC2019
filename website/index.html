What exactly is netLink?

Do you often find yourself looking for things to do? Are you tired of doing the same things every weekend? netLink is a great platform to connect you with others in your area who share similar interests. Maybe you love playing tennis but can never find anyone to play with, or maybe you would love to go see a movie but none of your friends are interested. netLink will help you match up your desired activities with others who are also interested in doing the same things.
